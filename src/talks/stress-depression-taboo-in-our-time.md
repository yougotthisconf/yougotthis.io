  title: Stress & Depression – a Taboo in our Time
  abstract: Stress and stress-induced depression hit many knowledge workers, and yet it is still a taboo. “I am stressed” has become something we hear every day, and it has almost become prestigious to say so; it shows that we are busy, important people. On the other hand, it is a bit embarrassing to be really stressed and not being able to handle it.<br /><br />In this talk, I will discuss the taboo and explore why it hits knowledge workers so often, as well as come with tips and tricks to prevent it.
