  title: Understanding & Looking After Your Mental Health

  abstract: “I know how to look after my physical health, but I don’t know how to look after my mental health.”<br /><br />This question is one that sparked the start of my talking about mental health and self care. During my time living with anxiety and C-PTSD, I’ve learned a lot about self care and looking after my mental health, and I knew I could share my strategies with people. This talk is about what self care is, why it’s important regardless of your mental health status, and how to use self care as a mood barometer. I’ll be sharing tricks and tips I use to keep my own mental health stable, and how to adapt them to find your own self care routine.
